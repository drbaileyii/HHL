HAND GRADING & CLEANING

(Add On Service) - We provide the service of stripping out old grass or turf by hand and hauling it away. This is followed by raking and smoothing out existing soils or by adding additional soils for proper gradient.

FILL DIRT AND TOP SOIL

(Stand Alone/Add-on) - This includes the labor of manually wheel barreling the dirt/soil to the exact site such as a flower bed or garden area. Also includes area clean-up of any loose dirt left behind. We may have a min amount required if this is preformed as a stand alone service.

MULCH

(Stand Alone/Add-on) You will receive one yard of landscape Natural Premium mulch delivered, dumped, wheeled, and spread at your desired location. [One yard of mulch covers approximately 27 cubic feet or area.(9ft. length x 3ft. width x 1ft. height)] Includes clean-up of any loose mulch left behind. Black Colored Mulch is typically 10.00 extra. Min amount is 3 yards as a stand alone service.
